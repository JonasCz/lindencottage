Linden Cottage

A lovely place to be in the south of france

About

This beautiful 400 year old stone cottage in the quiet hamlet of Les Groleaux in St Astier de Duras is the perfect place to relax from the demands of our fast-paced way of living. While the house maintains it's old features it has been modernized to provide a home from home experience. It has three spacious bedrooms one of which is ensuite. They all have double beds, while one of them has two single beds as well. The house is set on its own grounds with about an acre of well tended land. There are lots of shaded areas under trees to sit or lie around in a hammock. The house is surrounded by a terrace with some covered areas to make for enjoyable outdoor eating.

For those of you who enjoy the meditative life there is also a beautiful spacious meditation room overlooking a lily-pond. The house is situated about 7km from Plum Village, the renowned Zen Centre of Thich Nhat Hanh.

There is a small swimming pool to cool you down during the lazy hot summer days. For the colder days there is under-floor heating and a wood burning stove offering a cosy atmosphere throughout.

Wifi is available and there is ample parking.

Some useful information

  • Nearest airports: Bordeaux (100km) and Bergerac (30km)
  • Nearest towns: Duras (8km), Eymet (15km) and Ste Foy-La-Grande (18km) with regular weekly markets and good restuarants.
  • For walkers, there are excellent local randonnées with printed guides available.
  • Horse riding and cycling: Horse riding facilities are available locally and bicycles can be rented in Duras.

Further information can be supplied to you on request.
