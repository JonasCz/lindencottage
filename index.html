Charming Cottage for Sale close to Plum Village



About

If you are hoping to relocate close to Plum village this may be just the place for you. Our 400-year-old stone cottage with its adjoining meditation space is a true sanctuary. There is also a large outbuilding which is currently used as storage space and it is possible with planning permission to convert into a substantial home.

There are 3 bedrooms with one en suite bedroom, and an upstairs bathroom. A large kitchen and dining area with a separate lounge. The house is decorated to a high standard with beautiful stained-glass windows throughout and is in turn-key condition.

The garden is large and well-tended with both a lilly-pond and above ground swimming pool and lots of trees throughout the property.

The house is for sale for €330,000

Some useful information

  • Nearest airports: Bordeaux (100km) and Bergerac (30km)
  • Nearest railway station: Ste Foy-La-Grande (18km), with regular service to/from Bordeaux and Bergerac.
  • Markets and Restaurants: There are plenty of good local markets and restaurants; as well as in Ste Foy-La-Grande, other examples include the towns of Duras (8km) and Eymet (15km).
  • Car Hire: As well as at the airports, cars can be hired locally.
  • For walkers, there are excellent local randonnées with printed guides available.
  • Horse riding and cycling: Horse riding facilities are available locally and bicycles can be rented in Duras.
